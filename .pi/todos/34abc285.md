## Problem
There's no `arad status` command to give a quick summary of the project's ARAD state — how many entities of each type, how many relationships, how many issues. The `arad check` command is for finding problems, but there's no lightweight "at a glance" view.

## What should happen
`arad status` should show:
```
ARAD project "my-project"
  15 requirements (12 accepted, 2 draft, 1 deprecated)
  8 assumptions (5 validated, 2 unvalidated, 1 invalidated)
  20 decisions (14 accepted, 3 proposed, 3 superseded)
  42 relationships
  ⚠ 2 unvalidated assumptions backing decisions
  ✓ No contradictions
```

New file: `src/commands/status.ts`
