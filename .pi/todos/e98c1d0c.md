## Risk Entity Type

Add `risk` as a new entity type (prefix `K-`) with mitigation relationships.

### What
- New entity type: `risk` with prefix `K`, folder `risks/`
- Statuses: `identified`, `mitigated`, `accepted`, `materialized`, `closed`
- Template: description, likelihood, impact, mitigation strategy
- New relationships: `exposed_to`, `mitigates`, `if_wrong_becomes`
- `arad add risk "Payment provider downtime during peak hours"`
- `arad trace` / `arad impact` traverse risk links
- `arad check` warns about unmitigated risks, risks linked to invalidated assumptions

### Why
Risks are currently buried in assumption bodies or decision consequences. First-class risks let you ask "what's our exposure?" and distinguish risk-mitigation decisions from feature decisions.

### Files to touch
- `src/types.ts` — `Risk` interface, `ENTITY_CONFIG` entry, new edge types
- `src/graph/graph.ts` — handle risk edges
- `src/io/parser.ts` — parse risk fields
- `src/io/files.ts` — risk folder support
- `src/commands/add.ts` — risk type
- `src/commands/check.ts` — unmitigated risk warnings
- Tests
