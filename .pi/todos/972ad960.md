## Stakeholder Entity Type

Add `stakeholder` as a new entity type (prefix `S-`) with relevant relationships.

### What
- New entity type: `stakeholder` with prefix `S`, folder `stakeholders/`
- Statuses: `active`, `inactive`
- Template: description, role/responsibilities, contact info
- New relationships: `requested_by`, `affects`, `believed_by`
- `arad add stakeholder "Warehouse operations team"`
- `arad trace` / `arad impact` traverse stakeholder links
- Stakeholders show in `arad check` as "requirements with no stakeholder"

### Why
Fills the "who" gap. Trace/impact becomes meaningful when you can see who cares about a decision or who'd be affected by a change.
